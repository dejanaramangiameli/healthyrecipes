Falafel Salad

For the salad:

  • 3 cups iceberg lettuce
  • 3 cups spring mix
  • 1/3 cucumber, sliced
  • 5 cherry tomatoes, sliced
  • 3 falafel balls, baked
  • 1 tbsp hummus
  • 1/4 turnip, diced
  • 1/2 carrot, sliced
  • 3 tbsp black olives, sliced

For the dressing:

  • 2 tbsp tahini paste
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced (1 tbsp juice)
  • 1 tbsp cold water
  • 2 tbsp dried parsley flakes
  • 1 tsp garlic, finely minced
  • 1/2 tsp salt
